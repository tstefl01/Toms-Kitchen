steps:
  - title: Prep Chicken
    desc: "Slice **4 chicken breasts** into flat, even pieces for consistent cooking and max crust surface."
  - title: Marinade
    desc: "Mix dashi, chicken salt, sriracha & water. Coat chicken, cover, refrigerate **2 hours**."
  - title: Set Up Dredges
    desc: "Combine dry dredge in one large bowl. Whisk buttermilk, water & egg whites in a second. Heat oil."
  - title: Build the Base
    desc: "Stir *100g dry dredge* into the chicken & marinade. Creates the craggy clumps that anchor the crust."
  - title: "First Dry \u2014 *60\xD7*"
    desc: "Toss in the dry dredge bowl *sixty times*. Tap off excess. Builds the shaggy layered coating."
  - title: Wet Dip
    desc: "Into wet dredge and **straight back out**. Tap off excess liquid immediately."
  - title: "Second Dry \u2014 *60\xD7*"
    desc: "Back into dry dredge, *sixty more times*. This second pass locks in the layers and seals the crust."
  - title: Fry
    desc: "Oil to **170\xB0C**. Fry **8\u20139 min** until deeply golden. Work in batches."
  - title: Finish & Serve
    desc: "Drain, *dust immediately* with chicken salt while hot. Serve with waffles and maple syrup."
