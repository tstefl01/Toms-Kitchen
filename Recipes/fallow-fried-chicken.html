Method
1
Prep Chicken
Slice 4 chicken breasts into flat, even pieces for consistent cooking and max crust surface.
2
Marinade
Mix dashi, chicken salt, sriracha & water. Coat chicken, cover, refrigerate 2 hours.
3
Set Up Dredges
Combine dry dredge in one large bowl. Whisk buttermilk, water & egg whites in a second. Heat oil.
4
Build the Base
Stir 100g dry dredge into the chicken & marinade. Creates the craggy clumps that anchor the crust.
5
First Dry — 60×
Toss in the dry dredge bowl sixty times. Tap off excess. Builds the shaggy layered coating.
6
Wet Dip
Into wet dredge and straight back out. Tap off excess liquid immediately.
7
Second Dry — 60×
Back into dry dredge, sixty more times. This second pass locks in the layers and seals the crust.
8
Fry
Oil to 170°C. Fry 8–9 min until deeply golden. Work in batches.
9
Finish & Serve
Drain, dust immediately with chicken salt while hot. Serve with waffles and maple syrup.
